About

Hi, I am Oishy Saha. Welcome to my profile! I am a third year Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Maryland, College Park. I am currently working as a Graduate Research Assistant at the DSPCAD Lab under Dr. Shuvra S. Bhattacharyya. My research focuses on applied machine learning, signal processing, embedded systems, wireless networks, and hardware/software co-design. My long-term goal is to design intelligent, resource-efficient ML systems that can operate on constrained hardware to enable reliable, real-time decision-making at the edge.

Prior to joining UMD, I served as a lecturer in the Military Institute of Science and Technology (MIST) in the department of EECE. I earned my B.Sc degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology in 2022 with Honors. My undergaduate thesis project was advised by Dr. Shaikh Anowarul Fattah. The project centered on emotion recognition using multi-channel electroencephalogram (EEG) signals using deep learning and signal processing techniques.
