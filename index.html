About

I am a third year Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Maryland, College Park. I am currently working as a Graduate Research Assistant at the DSPCAD Lab under Dr. Shuvra S. Bhattacharyya. My research focuses on applied machine learning, signal processing, embedded systems, wireless sensor networks, and hardware/software co-design. My long-term goal is to design intelligent, resource-efficient ML systems that can operate on constrained hardware to enable reliable, real-time decision-making at the edge.

Before joining UMD, I served as a lecturer in the Military Institute of Science and Technology (MIST) in the department of EECE. I earned my B.Sc degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology in 2022.
