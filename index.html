About

I am a third year Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Maryland, College Park. I am currently working as a Graduate Research Assistant at the DSPCAD Lab under Dr. Shuvra Bhattacharyya. My research focuses on applied machine learning, signal processing, embedded systems, wireless sensor networks, and hardware/software co-design. Previously, I served as a lecturer in the Military Institute of Science and Technology. I graduated from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology in 2022.
